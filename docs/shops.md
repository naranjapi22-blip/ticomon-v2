# Shops

`!shop` opens three candy shops: Technology, Fossil Lab, and Pastry Shop.
Purchases use type-specific candies and create a normal creature with a new
collection number, random nature, generated IVs and size, no shiny status, and
no minted nature.

Technology includes Porygon, Golett, Rotom, and the confirmed Rotom Heat, Wash,
and Mow variants. Rotom Frost and Fan are temporarily unavailable because a
canonical persisted variant and matching graphic have not been confirmed.

The Fossil Lab contains the fossil catalogue. Pastry Shop supports the 45
canonical Alcremie combinations (9 creams × 5 decorations): random combination
for 80 Fairy, selected cream with random decoration for 120 Fairy, and fully
selected combination for 160 Fairy. Only Pastry Shop shows the exact animated
preview before confirmation; missing graphics use the normal fallback and do
not block a purchase.

Candies are deducted together with creature creation in one transaction.
Prices are initial and may be adjusted after real usage data is available.
